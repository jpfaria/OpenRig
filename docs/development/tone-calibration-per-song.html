Tone Doctor — every song we measured

The genre limits are a p90 summary; this is the raw material behind them. Each reference stem (isolated guitar, lead & rhythm) measured by OpenRig's own analyzer, grouped by the genre it was labeled as.

Read it this way: the spread within a genre is the story the summary hides — e.g. how far apart grunge's fizz-heavy takes sit, or whether a song labeled "clean" actually measures dark. harsh and clip are ~0 across this corpus (dark, non-clipping masters), so the bars show mud, fizz and boom; every value is in the hover and the table.
